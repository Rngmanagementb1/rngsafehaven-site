Safe Transitional ADU Housing for Unhoused Women

The Safe Haven ADU Initiative, developed by RNG Management, is designed to create safe transitional housing through Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs) for unhoused women who are ready to rebuild their lives.

Our goal is to combine secure housing with practical support services including financial literacy, employment readiness, case management, personal development, and permanent housing placement assistance.

The Need

Many unhoused women face serious barriers including domestic violence, income instability, displacement, and lack of affordable housing. Emergency shelters are often temporary and overcrowded. ADUs offer a practical, community-based solution that provides privacy, safety, and stability.

Who We Serve

Unhoused Women

Women ages 25–40 who need stable transitional housing and support.

Women Rebuilding Stability

Participants who are employed or actively seeking employment.

Women Seeking a Fresh Start

Women committed to personal growth, safety, and permanent housing.

Program Services

  • Safe ADU transitional housing
  • Financial literacy education
  • Employment readiness support
  • Case management and referrals
  • Personal improvement classes
  • Permanent housing placement assistance
